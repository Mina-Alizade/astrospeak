Tutorial
Someone's stuck on the installation step
A participant raises their hand — the software isn't installing. Others are watching. What do you say?
Useful phrases
▸"No problem at all — let's take a look together."
▸"This sometimes happens on Windows — try running it as administrator."
▸"While we sort this out, everyone else can move on to step three."
▸"Could you share your screen with me?"
Conference
Introducing yourself to a senior researcher
You're at the coffee break. A well-known astronomer is standing nearby. You make your move.
Useful phrases
▸"Excuse me — I'm Mina Alizadeh, PhD candidate from Iran. I really enjoyed your talk."
▸"I work on long-period variable stars in M33 — there's actually some overlap with your work on..."
▸"I'd love to get your thoughts on this when you have a moment."
Your Talk
Handling a tough question after your talk
Someone asks a question you don't know the exact answer to. The room is quiet.
Useful phrases
▸"That's a really good question — I haven't fully explored that angle yet."
▸"My initial feeling is... but I'd want to look at the data more carefully."
▸"Could we perhaps discuss this further after the session?"
